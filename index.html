PopUp: Sell by Weight

Shopify POS doesn't support selling by weight. PopUp fixes that — and keeps everything in one place, no Square required.

  • → In person weight-based and by-item selling
  • → On-device catalog loads instantly, even with no signal at the market
  • → CSV import/export for on-device catalogs
  • → Forgot something? Add products from your phone on market day
  • → Every sale syncs to Shopify automatically — one report, one receipt
